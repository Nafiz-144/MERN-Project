About Me

Professional Background

I'm a dedicated Embedded Systems Engineer passionate about designing and developing reliable firmware solutions that bridge hardware and software. With expertise in bare-metal programming, microcontroller architecture, and real-time embedded systems, I transform complex hardware requirements into efficient, scalable embedded solutions.

Expertise & Specialization

My journey combines deep knowledge of STM32 and ESP32 microcontrollers, IoT integration, and AI-powered systems. I specialize in low-level firmware development, communication protocol implementation (UART, SPI, I2C, MQTT), and building intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. From smart agriculture to AI-powered retail, I'm committed to pushing the boundaries of embedded systems innovation.

Beyond Development

When I'm not working on embedded projects, I'm exploring new microcontroller platforms, optimizing code for performance, contributing to open-source embedded projects, or researching cutting-edge IoT technologies. I believe in continuous learning and sharing knowledge with the embedded systems community.
